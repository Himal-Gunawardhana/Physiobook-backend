👑 Chapter 1: The Super Admin
Step 1.1: Login to Platform POST /auth/login
Log in with the hardcoded master account to gain global creation rights.
Step 1.2: Establish a Clinic POST /clinics
Create a new clinic entity on the database. Requires super admin rights.
Step 1.3: Assign exactly ONE Clinic Admin POST /staff
Create an admin account permanently bound to the clinic ID we just created.
Step 1.4: Step Down (Logout)
The Super Admin's job is done. Clear the token.
🏢 Chapter 2: The Clinic Admin
Step 2.1: Login as Clinic Admin POST /auth/login
Sarah logs in to manage ONLY her Miami Sports clinic.
Step 2.2: Add Treatment Service POST /clinics/:id/services
Define what patients can book at Sarah's clinic.
Step 2.3: Hire a Therapist POST /staff
Hire John the Therapist. (Notice Sarah doesn't need to pass a clinicId—the backend derives it from her token securely).
Step 2.4: Step Down (Logout)
🩺 Chapter 3: The Therapist
Step 3.1: Login as Therapist POST /auth/login
Step 3.2: Configure Availability PUT /staff/therapists/:id/schedule
John tells the system he works weekdays 09:00 to 17:00. This fuels the slot generator.
Step 3.3: Step Down (Logout)
📅 Chapter 4: The Patient
Step 4.1: Register Account POST /auth/register
A random person from the internet decides to sign up. They will receive an email.
Step 4.2: Verify Email Token GET /auth/verify-email
Check Mailhog (http://localhost:8025) for your token and paste it here, OR just paste the entire link from the email!
Step 4.3: Login as Patient POST /auth/login
Now that they are verified, log them in.
Step 4.4: See Open Appointments GET /bookings/slots
Jane queries the service system to automatically find 45-minute blocks tomorrow that John is available to book.
Step 4.5: Claim a Slot! POST /bookings
